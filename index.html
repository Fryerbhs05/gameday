Conflicted reads your rosters across every league you play, watches the scores, and tells you exactly who to cheer for in real time.

How it works
01
Connect Sleeper or Yahoo
Sleeper is a username. Yahoo is a single OAuth click. We don't store credentials.
02
Rosters and matchups load
Every league, every opponent lineup, pulled automatically for the current week.
03
Players get classified
Cheer for, conflicted, or enemy — based on where each one shows up across your leagues.
04
Allegiances shift live
Already clinched a league? Players there go neutral. Still in it? Every point matters.
